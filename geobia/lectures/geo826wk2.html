Week 2

Learning Objectives:

  • Explain the goal of geographic science
  • Describe the process of remote sensing from measurement to knowledge creation
  • Compare and contrast three approaches to knowledge creation- visual, pixel-based, and image-based analysis
  • Perform the steps necessary to open an image in Python
